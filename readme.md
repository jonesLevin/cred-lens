# CRED LENS - A Smart System that Helps Financial Institutions Grow

`A smart system that uses many kinds of information to decide whether people with little or no credit history can get loans, while helping financial institutions safely grow their business.`

Imagine a bank is trying to decide whether or not to lend money to people. Usually, the bank looks at the borrowers history, refered to as the borrowers credit file.

## What are we Doing?

In this project, we focus on **thin-file borrowers** (Borrowers whose credit file has little or no history). These may be young people who have gotten their first salaries, or have never used loans or credit cards before. The financial institutions therefore do not know much about them.

Instead of using only traditional credit history, this system uses other clues to decide if someone is trustworthy or not. These may include mobile money usage, paying electricity bills, shopping patterns, salary deposits e.t.c

## Data

The data in this project is synthetically generated and does not represent real data.

## Goal

Design a smart system that help financial institutions grow by:
 - finding good new customers
 - lending to people more safely
 - reducing bad loans
 - earning more money
